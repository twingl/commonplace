How it works

- Review | *give your day edges* | The anxiety that you're reading all these things, but none of it is really sinking in, is a familar one to many. Commonplace turns your day activties into diary entries; now you can revise what you collected today, and what you collected months prior.
- Connect | *see the bigger picture* | With all your things in one place, scerendipity is no longer the exception...
- Discuss | *continue the conversation* | You shouldn't have to end a conversation simply because you've moved onto something else. Commonplace lifts your conversations from the margins, and enables you to contine the discussion under today's light.
